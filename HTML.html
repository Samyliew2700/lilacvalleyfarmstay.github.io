BOOK YOUR STAY

Lilac Valley Barn

Lilac Valley Barn is a high-end, modern take-on farm-stay accommodation. This gorgeous farmhouse has three bedrooms: a master bedroom with a king-size bed and two guest rooms, one with a queen-size bed and the other with two single beds. There are two fancy bathrooms both with showers, basins and toilets with a rustic minimalist design, only the main bathroom has a bathtub. Also has spacious relaxing areas like the living room and kitchen, this house can easily fit up to six guests or more. Wireless internet is also available for guests and a patio with a perfect view of the beautiful Blue Mountains scenery.
